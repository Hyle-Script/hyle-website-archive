A new way to
create & share
After Effects content.

Hyle enables you to generate content from a simple and understandable syntax.


Readable

Hyle's syntax is focused on elegance and readability. What you read is really what
you'll get.


Shareable

Sharing text is easy. Save it as a gist, send it
as an email, tweet it or even fax it (but please don't).


Fast

Copy, paste, process. Or parse a standalone file. Either way, stay in After Effects and forget the binary ffx files.
